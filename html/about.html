Bihar Buddham Martial Arts Club is a dedicated martial arts training academy focused on building discipline, physical fitness, self-defense skills, and strong character in students of all age groups.

The club is founded and led by Martial Rajan, a Black Belt Martial Artist, National Gold Medalist, and National Referee, ensuring training is conducted with recognized standards, safety, and correct techniques.

Our club provides structured and progressive martial arts training that helps students:
  • Develop strength, flexibility, and stamina
  • Learn practical self-defense techniques
  • Build discipline, confidence, and respect
  • Prepare for belt examinations and competitions
Training programs are designed for kids, teens, and adults, with emphasis on proper fundamentals, safety, and continuous improvement.

At Bihar Buddham Martial Arts Club, martial arts is taught not only as a sport, but as a way to build focus, discipline, and a healthy lifestyle.
